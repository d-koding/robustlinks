Robust Links V2 Demo

This page demonstrates how the RobustLinksV2 library renders pre-configured robust links — links that have been explicitly annotated by the page author with data-originalurl and data-versiondate attributes.

Robust Links annotations are always the deliberate choice of the page author. This library renders and parses annotations you provide — it does not automatically generate or guess annotations on your behalf. See hvdsomp.info/robustlinks/#examples for the full range of authoring scenarios.
